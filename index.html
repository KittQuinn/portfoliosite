About Sean

I Originally wanted to pursue a career in the entertainment industry. In the summer of 2000, I made a living as a professional firespinner, mostly performing in nightclubs, art shows, and private parties. From there I went on to DJ for half a year in Florida, before returning to Austin as a Texas Hold'em dealer.

Not wanting to burn out on the entertainment field, I decided to put my love of the internet to good use, so I started pursuing a career in software technology, and obtained a job with a small webhost by the name of ApolloHosting, through the help of my roommate who worked there.

Having spent approximately 10 years in webhosting technical support across multiple hosts (from ApolloHosting to Hostgator to WPEngine) I am now ready to begin my journey into frontend-development by mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
